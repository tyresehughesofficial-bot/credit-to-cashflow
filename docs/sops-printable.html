Standard Operating Procedure

Credit Repair & Building

Clean and strengthen a client's profile until they are fundable.

Owner: Credit SpecialistSystem: Credit + Clientv1.0
  1. Intake. Client Command Center → Import Client (MyFreeScoreNow CSV or manual).
  2. Diagnose. Diagnosis tab — read health band, main problems, AI recommendations.
  3. Place in the journey. The Prolific Method → pick client → it auto-places them in Repair or Rebuild. Read the AI Strategist.
  4. Send Round 1. Repair phase generates dispute letters (one per bureau) — Copy, send certified mail, log in Round Tracking.
  5. Track items. Negative Account Tracking — update each item's status as bureaus respond (open → disputing → deleted).
  6. Escalate. CFPB Center — file complaints for items "verified" without proof; advance to Round 2/3.
  7. Rebuild. Builder accounts, utilization under 10%, add positive tradelines.
Done when: scores reach Good/Excellent and utilization is low → client is ready for Funding.

Business Funding

Get the cleaned-up client approved for capital.

Owner: Credit SpecialistSystem: Fundingv1.0
  1. Check readiness. Approval Readiness — work the "Almost Ready" list (score 640+, utilization ≤30%, ≤4 inquiries, 0 derogatories).
  2. Build structure. Prolific Method → Structure phase → Business-in-a-Box checklist: LLC, EIN, address, phone, website, business bank, operating agreement, lender docs.
  3. Position. Prolific → Position phase — freeze inquiries, build bank relationships, align personal + business credit.
  4. Execute the sequence. Funding Engine + Fund phase — stack 0% business cards same-day → lines of credit → loans.
  5. Track the money. Log the funding fee in Finance · Wealth Map; log the deal in CRM → Deals.
Done when: capital is approved → move the client to the Grow phase.

Running the Company

The business runs on the system, not on the founder.

Owner: AdministratorSystem: Operationsv1.0
  1. Daily. Open the Command Center → work the Alerts panel (urgent clients, funding-ready, pending payments).
  2. Sales. CRM → Pipeline — move every lead through the 10 stages; "Send & log" follow-ups; "Create payment link" to close.
  3. Money (weekly). Finance · Wealth Map — log revenue/expenses/payouts; watch net profit + top offers; pay affiliates from the Affiliate System.
  4. Team. Team OS — Mon alignment, Wed training, Fri review; each person has a role + KPIs.
  5. Questions. Operations Hub — SOP Library, Training Hub, Contracts, IP Library.
  6. Automations. Set trigger → action rules; the scheduler runs them every 15 minutes.
The throughline: every client flows Diagnose → Repair → Rebuild → Structure → Position → Fund → Grow — run from one dashboard.
